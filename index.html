The Game

Enter the Infinite Castle

A dungeon with no ceiling. Every run is different. Every death leaves a mark. Every triumph is yours — permanently on-chain.

One Castle. Infinite Stories.

Choose your champion from a roster of 100+ heroes, each with a unique 50+ skill tree. Enter the Void Castle — a dungeon that stretches infinitely upward. Each floor is a new text-driven encounter with monsters, traps, merchants, and mysteries your opponents haven't faced.

Combat is calculated, not mashed. Your build, your decisions, your timing — all matter. Die, and your run ends. But your on-chain items survive. Your cosmetics persist. Your reputation in the leaderboard stands.

Every 10 floors a Boss looms — massively powerful encounters that require the right build and strategy to overcome. Between floors, random events scatter: a wandering merchant, a cursed altar, a prisoner with intel, a collapsed floor revealing a secret wing.

Turn-Based Text CombatChoose your actions each turn — Attack, Skill, Item, Flee. Status effects, combos, and build synergies reward mastery.
